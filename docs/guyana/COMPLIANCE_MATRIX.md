## 1. Universal Requirements (All Businesses)

These requirements apply to **ALL** businesses operating in Guyana, regardless of type, size, or industry.

| Requirement | Agency | Frequency | Deadline | Mandatory/Optional | Penalty for Non-Compliance |
|-------------|--------|-----------|----------|-------------------|---------------------------|
| **Business Registration** | DCRA | Once (at startup) | Before operations | Mandatory | Cannot legally operate; fines |
| **TIN Registration** | GRA | Once (at startup) | Before taxable activity | Mandatory | Cannot conduct business; penalties |
| **Trade License** | GRA | Annually | Renewal date | Mandatory | Fines; business closure |
| **Business Name Renewal** | DCRA | Periodic (verify cycle) | Renewal date | Mandatory | Loss of business name |

## 2. Business Type-Specific Requirements

### A. Sole Proprietorship (Self-Employed Individual)

| Requirement | Agency | Frequency | Deadline | Documents Required |
|-------------|--------|-----------|----------|-------------------|
| Business Name Registration | DCRA | Once | 3 working days | ID, proof of address |
| TIN | GRA | Once | Same day | DCRA certificate, ID |
| NIS Self-Employed Registration | NIS | Once | ~1 week | DCRA cert, TIN |
| NIS Contributions | NIS | Quarterly/Annually | Verify with NIS | Self-employment form |
| Individual Income Tax Return | GRA | Annually | April 30 | Income statements, receipts |
| Capital Gains Tax (if applicable) | GRA | Annually | April 30 | Computation sheet |
| Trade License | GRA | Annually | Renewal date | DCRA cert, TIN, permits |

**Employee Threshold:**
- If hiring employees, must register as employer with NIS
- Must register for PAYE with GRA

### D. Non-Profit / NGO (Company Limited by Guarantee)

| Requirement | Agency | Frequency | Deadline | Documents Required |
|-------------|--------|-----------|----------|-------------------|
| Company Incorporation | DCRA | Once | 1-2 weeks | Memorandum (no share capital), objectives |
| TIN | GRA | Once | 1 day | Incorporation certificate |
| Tax-Exempt Status Application (if applicable) | GRA | Once | Before claiming exemption | Constitution, objectives proof |
| NIS Employer Registration (if hiring) | NIS | Once | ~1 week | Incorporation docs |
| PAYE (for employees) | GRA | Monthly | 14th | Payroll records |

**Special Notes:**
- May be exempt from income tax if registered charity
- Still must register for TIN

## 3. Frequency Matrix

### One-Time (At Business Start)

| Requirement | Agency | Processing Time | Fee (GYD) |
|-------------|--------|----------------|-----------|
| Business Name Registration | DCRA | 3 working days | $6,000 |
| Company Incorporation | DCRA | 1-2 weeks | Contact DCRA |
| TIN Application | GRA | 1 day | Free |
| VAT Registration (if applicable) | GRA | 3-5 days | Free |
| NIS Employer Registration | NIS | ~1 week | Free |
| Trade License (first time) | GRA | 1-2 weeks | Varies by type |

### Monthly

| Requirement | Agency | Deadline | Late Penalty |
|-------------|--------|----------|--------------|
| VAT Return | GRA | 21st of following month | $1,000/day or 10% (whichever greater) |
| PAYE Return (Form 2) | GRA | 14th of each month | Penalties + 2%/month interest |
| Withholding Tax Return | GRA | 14th of each month | Penalties + 2%/month interest |
| NIS Contribution Schedule | NIS | Verify (typically mid-month) | Penalties + interest |

### Quarterly

| Requirement | Agency | Deadline | Notes |
|-------------|--------|----------|-------|
| Estimated Tax Payments | GRA | Quarterly (verify) | If applicable to business type |
| Self-Employed NIS (possible) | NIS | Verify with NIS | May be quarterly or annual |

### Annually

| Requirement | Agency | Deadline | Late Penalty |
|-------------|--------|----------|--------------|
| NIS Annual Return | NIS | January 31 | Penalties, non-compliance status |
| Corporate Tax Return | GRA | April 30 | Penalties + 2%/month interest |
| Individual Income Tax Return | GRA | April 30 | Penalties + 2%/month interest |
| PAYE Annual Return (Form 5) | GRA | April 30 | Penalties |
| Capital Gains Tax | GRA | April 30 (with IT return) | Penalties + interest |
| DCRA Annual Return | DCRA | Verify deadline | Notice of default, strike-off |
| Beneficial Ownership Update | DCRA | Annually or when changes | Notice of default |
| Trade License Renewal | GRA | Renewal anniversary | Fines, inability to operate |
| Business Name Renewal | DCRA | Renewal cycle | $2,500 | Loss of business name |

## 5. Penalty Matrix

### GRA Penalties

| Violation | Penalty | Additional Consequences |
|-----------|---------|------------------------|
| **Late VAT Filing** | $1,000/day OR 10% of tax owed (whichever greater), max = tax owed | Non-compliance status; cannot get tax clearance |
| **Late VAT Payment** | 2% per month of outstanding tax | Compounds monthly |
| **Knowingly Fail to Submit VAT** (Criminal) | Fine up to $15,000 + 3 months imprisonment | Criminal record |
| **Late PAYE Filing/Payment** | Penalties + 2% per month interest | Non-compliance status |
| **Late Withholding Tax** | Penalties + 2% per month interest | Non-compliance status |
| **Late Corporate Tax** | Penalties + 2% per month interest | Non-compliance status |
| **Late Income Tax** | Penalties + 2% per month interest | Cannot get compliance certificate |
| **Operating Without TIN** | Fines + inability to conduct legal business | Business shutdown |
| **Operating Without Trade License** | Fines + business closure order | Legal action |

### NIS Penalties

| Violation | Penalty | Additional Consequences |
|-----------|---------|------------------------|
| **Late NIS Contributions** | Penalties + interest charges | Employees cannot claim benefits |
| **Failure to Register as Employer** | Fines + legal action | Employees not covered |
| **Deducting Employee Portion But Not Remitting** | Severe fines + legal action | Criminal charges possible |
| **Late Annual Return** | Penalties | Non-compliance status |

### DCRA Penalties

| Violation | Penalty | Additional Consequences |
|-----------|---------|------------------------|
| **Operating Without Business Registration** | Fines + shutdown | Cannot legally operate |
| **Late Annual Return** | Notice of Default | Company strike-off possible |
| **Failure to File Beneficial Ownership** | Notice of Default + fines | Company suspension |
| **Not Updating Director Changes** | Fines | Invalid company records |

## 7. Employee Count-Based Requirements

### 0 Employees (Self-Employed Only)

| Requirement | Frequency | Notes |
|-------------|-----------|-------|
| Individual Income Tax | Annually | April 30 |
| NIS Self-Employed Contributions | Quarterly/Annually | 12.5% of income |
| VAT (if applicable) | Monthly | If turnover ≥ $15M GYD |

### 1-5 Employees (Micro Business)

**Add:**
- NIS Employer Registration
- Monthly PAYE (Form 2)
- Monthly NIS Contribution Schedules
- Annual PAYE Return (Form 5)
- Annual NIS Return

### 6-20 Employees (Small Business)

**Same as 1-5, plus:**
- Consider payroll software for accuracy
- May qualify for small business incentives (check GRA)

### 21-50 Employees (Medium Business)

**Same as above, plus:**
- Likely required to have internal HR/payroll function
- May require audited financial statements

### 50+ Employees (Large Business)

**Same as above, plus:**
- Definitely require audited financial statements
- May have additional regulatory requirements depending on sector
- Consider professional tax/compliance services

## 8. Revenue-Based Requirements

### Revenue < $15,000,000 GYD Annually

| Requirement | Mandatory/Optional |
|-------------|--------------------|
| Business Registration (DCRA) | Mandatory |
| TIN (GRA) | Mandatory |
| VAT Registration | Optional (voluntary registration available) |
| Income Tax Filing | Mandatory |
| Trade License | Mandatory |
| NIS (if employer) | Mandatory |

### Revenue ≥ $15,000,000 GYD Annually

**Add:**
- **VAT Registration:** Mandatory
- **Monthly VAT Returns:** Mandatory
- **VAT Compliance:** Critical

### Revenue ≥ $50,000,000 GYD Annually

**Same as above, plus:**
- Likely require professional accounting services
- May require quarterly estimated tax payments
- May require audited financial statements

### Revenue ≥ $100,000,000 GYD Annually (Large Enterprise)

**Same as above, plus:**
- Definitely require audited financial statements
- May have sector-specific regulations
- May require corporate governance framework
- Consider dedicated compliance officer

## 10. Quick Compliance Checklists

### New Business Launch Checklist

- [ ] DCRA: Business name search and registration (3 days)
- [ ] GRA: TIN application (1 day)
- [ ] NIS: Employer registration if hiring (1 week)
- [ ] GRA: VAT registration if applicable
- [ ] GRA: Trade license application (1-2 weeks)
- [ ] Municipal: Business permit
- [ ] Sector licenses (if applicable)
- [ ] Bank account opening
- [ ] Insurance coverage
- [ ] **Timeline: 3-4 weeks total**

### Monthly Compliance Checklist

- [ ] Prepare payroll and calculate PAYE deductions
- [ ] Calculate NIS contributions (employee + employer portions)
- [ ] Compile VAT records (sales and purchases)
- [ ] 14th: File PAYE Return (Form 2) with GRA
- [ ] 14th: File Withholding Tax Return (if applicable)
- [ ] Mid-month: Submit NIS Contribution Schedule
- [ ] 21st: File VAT Return with GRA
- [ ] Pay all amounts due (PAYE, NIS, VAT)
- [ ] Retain proof of filing and payment

### Annual Compliance Checklist

- [ ] Prepare financial statements (Balance Sheet, P&L, Cash Flow)
- [ ] Arrange audit (if required)
- [ ] Jan 31: File NIS Annual Return
- [ ] Apr 30: File Corporate Tax Return (with financials)
- [ ] Apr 30: File PAYE Annual Return (Form 5)
- [ ] Apr 30: File Individual Income Tax (if applicable)
- [ ] Apr 30: File Capital Gains Tax (if applicable)
- [ ] File DCRA Annual Return (verify deadline)
- [ ] Update Beneficial Ownership (if changes)
- [ ] Renew trade license
- [ ] Renew business name (if due)
- [ ] Renew sector-specific licenses
